Reflector Selector

This project is nearing completion and will be available in the app store early 2020.

Game based learning activity developing emotional literacy and granularity. The app has Alan McLeans three phases of emotional development built in and the option to select specific emotions within each phase for focused work.

Specific teaching moments are easily identifiable and well resourced in the learner workflow in a compulsive entertaining format. Lavishly illustrated with artwork by Rhiannon van Muysen.
