whatMotivatesLearning.com

The whatmotivateslearning.com resource over the course of the last 15 years has delivered high quality research and development in the pedagogy of emotion, relationship, and self-image of all age groups.

Clients from all over the world and from all walks of life with an interest in teaching and learning in this important and emerging field have benefited from the non-profit basis that the site has adhered to.

whatMotivatesLearning.com are incredibly successful, but, as a non-profit, it's structure now restricts the growth and propegation of this important resource.

MADFullStack has been given the responsibiltiy to develop the next stage in development of the whatmotivateslearning.com resource.

This will involve the migration of the whatmotivateslearning service into the iOS app WML, and three separate objectives for the whatmotivateslearning.com site:

  • a fully monetised form with engaging interactive assets
  • a new lightweight scalable structure
  • achieve and overtake baseline PWA compliance
